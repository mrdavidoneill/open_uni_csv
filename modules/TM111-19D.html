    • Welcome to TM111 Introduction to computing and information technology 1, and, if this is the first module you have studied with The Open University, welcome to the University!

      This study planner will guide you through your studies week by week and remind you when essential things, such as assessments, should be done. Most of the study material is provided in the three printed books (one for each block) but there are also online activities to complete which you will find in the study planner.

      Please spend some time familiarising yourself with the contents and layout of this website, and visit it frequently as this is where news items and other announcements will be posted.

      We hope you enjoy the module and wish you good luck with your studies.

      Elaine Thomas (Module Chair) and the TM111 Module Team.
